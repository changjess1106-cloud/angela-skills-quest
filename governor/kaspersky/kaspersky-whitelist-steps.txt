KASPERSKY MANUAL WHITELIST STEPS

1. Open Kaspersky Settings.
2. Go to General settings -> Exclusions and types of detected objects.
3. Add scan exclusions for these folders:
 C:\Users\owner11\.openclaw\
 C:\workspace\angela-skills-quest\
 C:\Users\owner11\AppData\Roaming\npm\node_modules\openclaw\
4. Add trusted applications for:
 node.exe
 powershell.exe
 openclaw.cmd
 openclaw.mjs
5. If available, enable options similar to:
 Do not monitor application activity
 Do not monitor child application activity
 Do not inherit restrictions from parent process
6. Re-open OpenClaw and test:
 openclaw doctor
 openclaw health
 openclaw status
7. If local exclusions are blocked by policy, use administrator-managed Kaspersky policy instead.
